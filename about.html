Our Story

Creating Digital Lives.

Founded with a vision to transform the digital landscape, Minimo began as a small team of passionate developers in a shared workspace. Today, we’ve grown into a award-winning tech hub, delivering cutting-edge solutions to clients worldwide while maintaining our startup spirit.

2021

Launched with just 3 developers and a big dream. Delivered our first successful project for a local startup, proving our concept in the competitive tech market.

2023

Grew to 15 team members and secured our first enterprise client. Established our specialized training program to nurture junior talent in emerging technologies.

2024

Expanded our services to include AI solutions and opened our first international office.

2025

Launched our open-source initiatives. Partnered with leading tech universities to bridge the gap between education and industry needs.
